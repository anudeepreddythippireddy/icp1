Cricket

Cricket is a bat and ball game played between two teams. It was first played in England.


Rules of Cricket

  1. Each team contains 11 players.
  2. Bowler must bowl 6 legal deliveries to constitute a over.
  3. ODI cricket is played 50 overs.

Fielding Positions in Cricket

  • Wicket Kepper
  • Covers
  • Third Man
  • Long on
